\section{№ \textbf{6109}}

Дано:

$$R = 4 \text{м}, v = 6 \text{м/с}$$

Найти:

$$ a\text{ - ?} $$

Решение:

$$a = \omega^2 R $$
$$v = \omega R$$
$$\omega = \frac{v}{R}$$
$$a = \frac{v^2}{R}$$
$$a = \frac{36}{4} = 9$$

Ответ: \textbf{9}
